# 0.3.5

[+] New · [-] Removed · [*] Fix

## Title bar

- [-] Product logo removed from the system title bar — a cleaner look around the window controls and title.

## Navigation

- [*] The top navigation bar no longer jumps when the canvas toolbar is shown or hidden.
- [*] The canvas toolbar (world canvas / list mode) sits right below the main navigation, above your canvas.

## Editor & focus

- [+] You can drag the **left edge** of the right-hand info panel to resize it; your width is remembered.
- [+] **Focus mode** (default **F11**): fewer distractions while you write.

## @-mentions & connections

- [+] When you **@-mention** a note, you first pick the note, then the **kind of connection** — consistent with links in your text.
- [*] Editing no longer creates conflicting connection lines in the text.

## Version history

- [+] **Version history** (**F10**): earlier drafts of your note are kept; you can restore an older version (with a short heads-up first). How often snapshots are saved is controlled in **Settings → Performance**.

## Connections overview

- [+] The connections panel groups links by **type** and **color** — similar to how they appear on the canvas.
- [+] The **“Parent of”** relationship has its own column so it’s easy to spot.

## Dashboard

- [*] The **preview** on each project tile shows your canvas **with connection lines** — matching what you see when the project is open.

## World canvas

- [*] Connection lines are drawn **above** group areas so they stay readable and easy to click.
- [+] For images and text on the canvas: right-click **“Bring to front”** or **“Send to back”** — order is saved.
- [+] **Dragging connections** offers more handle options — including same-side handles, not only the classic out-to-in path.
- [*] **Large groups** move more smoothly when you drag them.
- [*] **List view:** assigning many notes to a group at once is noticeably faster.

## Views & saved views

- [+] **Views:** save favorite camera positions and snapshots of your canvas — handy for returning to a working view.
- [*] If **full editing in Views** is turned off, cards there are for viewing only (not draggable).


## World Canvas

- **Canvas bookmarks:** On the world canvas you can save named **camera positions** (pan + zoom) — separate from **Views**. **Group** expand/collapse is **not** stored. Panel at the bottom right; **Ctrl/Cmd+1–9** jumps to the 1st–9th entry in the sorted list; transition uses the same speed as **Performance → Views viewport transition**.
